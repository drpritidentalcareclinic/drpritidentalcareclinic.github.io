Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Priti Satish Shukla

BDS (MUHS) Dental Surgeon Family Dentist

Dr. Priti Satish Shukla is a highly skilled and compassionate dental surgeon with a BDS degree from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS). With years of dedicated practice, she has built a reputation for providing gentle, effective dental care for patients of all ages.
